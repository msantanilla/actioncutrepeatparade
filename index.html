ACTION! CUT! REPEAT!

–All Choreographed by Hollywood

of Loving Grace–

Los Angeles has been exploited by the film industry, which has used the city as a white canvas. Throughout its history it has been blasted, destroyed, underwater, rearranged, turned into hell, paradise, taken back in time and to the future, disguised as other cities, and given back to nature. Through this active set, all genres have been enacted by countless actors and stories. All of these have generated a plethora of reactions, phobias and emotions onto viewers. LA has conditioned and choreographed the world we live in.

I have been influenced by this set, even before I had ever lived in LA. Multiple fictions coexist through my body. Action! Cut! Repeat!, is a parade in which an avatar version of myself is choreographed to play several gestures and scenes from different films that are based in LA and have had a huge impact on me.

LA has come to me… many times, many ways.

Through a black room full of light and others alike...

Where logic dies while

singing on top of cars

sitting in the park

having lunch at the bulevars

mundane rites.

Sunny days

Rainy nights

Windy times...

At the beach,

…in the mountains.

I have lend my mind to LA

whom has installed and structured from romantic, dramatic,

anxious

and multiple other emotions.

Ingrained throughout my life

it has become a resident for life.

Always comes from multiple angles and times

A world in itself...

A different LA and, the same

loop;

aimed to universal collapse.

And now… I have come to LA…

where I have never stand but always been.

Out of sight.

Out of mind.

The body wants to manifest, recite and dance.

Unravel through the streets in search of sunny nights.
